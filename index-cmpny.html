About us

A fresh perspective on the delivery of technological solutions

Through a combination of first class advanced mechanical, structural, and vibration engineering, geospatial information intelligence, environmental technology, transport-geography, and emerging big-data analytics skills, we have the knowledge and experience to deliver a broad range of solutions to the oil and gas sector. Since 2009 we’ve been developing influential technology partnerships dedicated to helping our clients gain exposure to the right skill sets, services, and supplies. We have a range of MOU's with international corporations operating at the cutting edge of technology relevant to the Oil & Gas sector. We have worked extensively and successfully with both new and established businesses,  so are you ready to take your business to the next level?   2. Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE)
