Eusebio's CV

GIS & GPS Energy, Oil & Gas (Nig.) Limited (also known as G-GeoGas) is hinged on the concerted academic, working, and business experience of the Managing Director (MD) who is Dr Eusebio Odiari. These include a BSc in Mechanical Engineering with 1st Class (Ibadan), MSc (Imperial College) in Vibration Engineering, D.I.C., MSc (Cranfield) in Geographical Information Management, PhD research in non-linear dynamics of turbine rotor-stator interaction at Imperial College University of London, and PhD (Leeds) in Transport-Geography-Urban Mobility. During these academic and research activity, Eusebio was Foreign and Commonwealth Office (FCO) scholar, Shell International Postgraduate scholar, University of London Edmund Davis scholar, and University of Leeds 110 Anniversary scholar.


Eusebio has a wealth of experience on Rolls Royce Engines, researching on the non-linear dynamics of gas turbine rotor-stator interaction. Eusebio also worked on brake system NVH designs for Audi, Ford, Bugattti, and Daewoo. Eusebio has a wealth of working experience at T&N Technology (Ferodo Group), and at Lotus Cars & Engineering UK. Eusebio was Senior NVH Engineer at these establishments developing aero-engine and automotive components, and developing new automobile vehicles for the USA market. Eusebio conducted research at Cranfield University sponsored by ESRI on the Use of Geographical Information Systems (GIS) in Building Information Modelling (BIM). Such technology is used for smart construction site logistics Management. Eusebio was part of a team in the UK that incorporated anthropogenic and natural features in the revision of the entire UK Postcode Sector boundaries (PAF). Eusebio has conducted substantial Urban Mobility and Movement Patterns research, conducting big-data analytics, simulation and animation of individual railways passenger mobility. Such research uses UK consumer ticketing data to identify a repertory of individual passenger socio-demographic attributes and within rail-network experience of individual passengers.


Eusebio has a dual British and Nigerian citizenship, and this has facilitated substantial travels and work experience in USA (New York, Chicago, and California), China (Hebei Province, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Beijing, Xiamen, and Shanghai), Hong Kong, Japan, Thailand, Taiwan, Singapore, mainland Europe (Switzerland, Netherlands, Germany, Portugal, France, Spain, Italy, and Greece), United Kingdom, and sub-Sahara Africa (Ghana, Nigeria, Senegal, Gambia, Tanzania, Uganda, and Kenya). Eusebio’s strength is that he has developed a wealth of friendships and contacts over the years in academia and business, and many of these contacts now lead some of the largest corporations in sub-Sahara Africa, Europe, USA, and the Far East. On the strength of these, G-GeoGas under the management of Eusebio is poised to contribute to the technology needs of Oil & Gas facilities, and to the smart urban-growth needs of cities in their vicinity by applying his area of technical expertise.
